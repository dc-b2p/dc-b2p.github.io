A short list of authors who seldom include sex scenes in their writing

Struggling to find action/adventure books that will be accepted by the Beto and Hightower units? The following authors seldom include sex scenes in their writing, and are likely safe for units with very strict "no sex" policies. These lists were provided by a DCPL librarian:

Secular

  • Anthony Horowitz (author of the Alex Rider series)
  • Louis L'Amour
  • Brandon Sanderson
  • Lee Child
  • Mary Higgins Clark
  • Lis Wiehl
  • Noah Boyd

Authors who include Christian elements in their writing

  • Terri Blackstock
  • Randy Singer
  • Randy Alcorn
  • Erin Healy

If you know other authors who generally don't include sex scenes, please notify the webmaster so they can be added to the lists above.
