First-Class Mail

According to one post office, all rates remain the same as in 2019. That post office told us that standard envelopes weighing up to 1 ounce cost 55 cents, large envelopes weighing up to 1 ounce cost $1, additional ounces on both standard and large envelopes cost 15 cents, and postcards cost 35 cents. We have successfully mailed many large envelopes at that post office using that information. However, the information on the cost for additional ounces on large envelopes is contradicted by USPS's postage calculator and a second post office. The only possible interpretation of the rates shown by the postage calculator is that large envelopes cost $1 for the first ounce then 20 cents (not 15 cents) for each additional ounce.
