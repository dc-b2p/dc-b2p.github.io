Handouts

In addition to books, BtP sends various handouts, pamphlets and slips with information on how to get more resources on certain topics to inmates. 

These handouts are now located on the shelves that are relevant to their topics. They are in Green Paper Folders or Clear Plastic Magazine Holders (as seen in photos below). Below is a list of all of our current handouts (over 30) with their locations.  Please use the list, if you aren't sure where to find a certain handout. It will be in the room, as a resource as well.

None of the originals are on the shelves! They're kept safely in the "Files" metal cabinet in the closet instead. If you're taking the last copy or see that the handouts are running low, please ask Chris, Susan, Daria or Stephanie to make more copies upstairs.
