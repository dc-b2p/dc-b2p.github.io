Book Conditions

Mottled cover - okay for fair condition

Light brown, mostly even - okay for fair condition

Darker brown, uneven - do not send, in part because brown extends beyond cover onto regular page

Darker brown around the edges - do not send, in part because brown extends beyond cover onto regular page, and in part because binding appears damaged

Even browning - might be okay even for GC

Consistently brown except for an area (in the lower right corner) that is very white, with a sharp border between the two

Dark mottled brown, with a much lighter area in the center
