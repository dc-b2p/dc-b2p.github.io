--- title: "Postage Rates, Effective 7/10/2022" ---

[Last updated: July 12, 2022]

USPS increased its postage rates on July 10, 2022. Below is information on the new rates. We have verified the cost to send Media Mail packages of 1, 2, and 3 pounds by sending test packages. The other information is believed to be accurate, but due to previous instances of USPS sources providing inaccurate information, we lack full confidence in it. Please alert Maya and Jen if you discover any inaccuracies below.

Note 1: The underlying rule behind the rates listed above is that for packages weighing 1-7 pounds, the first pound costs $3.49 and each additional pound costs 67 cents. Warning: After the first 7 pounds, the rule changes and each additional pound costs 70 cents.

Note 2: In the table above, the third column shows suggested stamp combinations for using up leftover stamps.com postage that was printed at the August 2021 rates. The suggested combination for 3-pound packages uses postage stickers that used to be the correct postage for 2-pound packages. If you instead have $4.45 postage stickers that you want to get rid of, you can add four 10-cent stamps to get the current $4.83 postage for a 3-pound package (overpaying by 2 cents).

First-Class Mail

Letter-sized envelopes weighing up to one ounce now cost cost 60 cents to mail. Large envelopes (called "flats" by USPS) weighing up to one ounce cost $1.20 (two forever stamps) to mail. Additional ounces cost 24 cents regardless of the size of the envelope.

Note: Do not overstuff letter-sized envelopes. Letter-sized envelopes should weigh less than 3.5 ounces and be less than 1/4 inch thick. Exceeding these limits can cause an envelope to be considered a "parcel" rather than a "letter", resulting in drastic increases in cost.

The simplest way to pay for first-class mail is to apply the appropriate stamp(s) for the first ounce (i.e. one forever stamp for a letter-sized envelope or two forever stamps for a large envelope) then apply as many "additional ounce" stamps as are needed. Although BtP doesn't send postcards or international mail, we sometimes use "postcard forever" and "international forever" stamps. "Postcard forever" stamps are now worth 44 cents and "international forever" stamps are worth $1.40.
