--- title: "Links to lists of banned books" ---

Recent lists of banned books for some states can be obtained from The Marshall Project. Scroll down to the line that says "Which books are banned in [state name] prisons?" and select the state you want. Most lists appear to have been obtained in 2022.

Older lists, mostly dating to around 2019, can be obtained from two sources: Seattle's prison book program and the Human Rights Defense Center.

All of these lists are long, and can cause older smartphones and computers to choke. If you are capable of viewing only a short list, see the short list of popular books banned in Texas that was produced in 2019.
