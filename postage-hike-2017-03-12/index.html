POSTAGE HIKE 2017

Charity Hike for DC Books to Prisons


WHEN: Sunday, March 12, 2017 at 10:15 AM
WHERE: Grosvenor/Strathmore Metro Station â€” Meet by the bus bay, right outside the station at the newspaper bins.

Questions or optional RSVP can be sent to btopdc@gmail.com.

Come out for a special charity hike to raise postage money for DC Books to Prisons. The event will consist of a short hike, a lunch, and a trip to a used bookstore.

We will meet at 10:15 AM just outside the Grosvenor / Strathmore Metro Station on the Red Line, (Rockville / North Bethesda, MD) by the newspaper bins. Parking is free. Note that Metro runs less frequently on the weekend and is sometimes delayed due to track work.

Total distance will be about 5 miles. Please ensure that you are capable of walking 5 miles. This is a point-to-point hike. We will walk from the metro to Rock Creek Trail, and head north, ending up at Goldberg's Bagels. Featuring a broad menu of breakfast and lunch items and accolades for its bagel texture, we hope it will be a worthy replacement to our former lunch spot, Memsahib, which closed its doors after many years. After eating, we will adjourn to the Friends of the Montgomery County Library Bookstore, a book lover's paradise; shop for yourself or a prisoner. Finally, we will walk to White Flint Metro Station. If you drive, you can take the metro one stop south. Predicted end time is around 2:15 PM.

DC Books to Prisons is the beneficiary of this hike. Most funds raised by this charity are used for postage costs. Hundreds of requests pour in each month; the biggest limiting factor is postage money. The suggested donation amount is $5, which is the cost of sending 3 pounds of books to a prisoner.

**Please bring a donation for DC Books to Prisons, your SmartTrip card, cash for lunch ($13 + tax, tip, extras), and water. If you would like to make a donation online instead of bringing cash, that is fine. You can also make your donation in the form of self-adhesive postage stamps. Stamps worth $1.00 or $2.00 are especially appreciated.
