Contact Us

If you are contacting us to request books on behalf of a prisoner, please mail us the request. We do not have the systematic ability to respond to emailed requests. All requests should list subjects of interest and prison restrictions, if known. Requests by title or author are very challenging to fulfill.

Monetary donations are always appreciated. Checks should be made payable to "DC Books to Prisons" and sent to the address below. We also accept online donations .
