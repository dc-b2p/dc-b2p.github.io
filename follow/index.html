Stay Informed

If you are interested in Books to Prisons, please consider joining our announcements list and following us on Facebook and Twitter.

The Announcements List is a low-traffic mailing list intended to provide updates about Books to Prisons events, campaigns and projects. You can sign up for the Announcements List by going to groups.io/g/BtoPDC .

You can also keep informed and join discussions by following us on Facebook and Twitter .
