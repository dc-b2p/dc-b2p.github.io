--- title: "Stay Informed" ---

If you are interested in Books to Prisons, please consider joining our announcements list and following us on social media.

The Announcements List is a low-traffic mailing list intended to provide updates about DC Books to Prisons events, campaigns and projects. You can sign up for the Announcements List by going to groups.io/g/BtoPDC.

You can also keep informed and join discussions by following us on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram.
